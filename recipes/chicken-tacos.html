Chicken Tacos

Mom's Famous Chicken Tacos

If you like chicken tacos with a traditonal mexican style then these are for you! These tacos come packed with savory flavors combined with moms special ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts (1 pound, boneless and skinless)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Onion (1, diced)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Cumin (1 teaspoon)
  • Paprika (1 teaspoon)
  • Chili powder (1 teaspoon)
  • Salt and pepper (a pinch)
  • Lime juice (1 lime)
  • Corn or Flour tortillas (8-10)
  • Toppings: Chopped cilantro, diced onions, diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sliced avocado, salsa, sour cream, etc.

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with cumin, paprika, chili powder, salt, and pepper. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add the seasoned chicken breasts and cook for about 6-8 minutes on each side, or until fully cooked. Remove from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before shredding the chicken with two forks.
  2. In the same skillet, add a bit more olive oil if needed, and sauté the diced onion and minced garlic until softened.
  3. Return the shredded chicken to the skillet and toss with the sautéed onions and garlic. Squeeze lime juice over the chicken mixture and stir to combine. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  4. In a separate skillet or directly over a gas flame, warm the corn tortillas until they are soft and slightly charred.
  5. Fill each tortilla with a portion of the shredded chicken. Top with your choice of toppings, such as chopped cilantro, diced onions, diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, sliced avocado, salsa, and sour cream.
  6. Serve the tacos with some lime wedges on the side for an extra burst of flavor.
