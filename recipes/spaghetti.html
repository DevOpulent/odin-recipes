Spaghetti

Mom's Spaghetti is a family tradition for any household!

This spaghetti recipe comes from my Mom Ruby who is a master mind when it comes to pasta meals. Read the ingredients and follow the steps below and you will also have a taste of this spledid meal!

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti (1 pound)
  • Ground beef (1 pound)
  • Onion (1, diced)
  • Garlic (2 cloves, minced)
  • Rague sauce (3 cups)
  • Olive oil (2 tablespoons)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Italian seasoning (1 teaspoon)
  • Parmesan Cheese (dash)

Steps

  1. Boil a large pot of salted water. Add the spaghetti and cook according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add diced onion and minced garlic, saut√© until softened. Add ground beef, cook until browned. Drain any excess fat. Stir in marinara sauce and Italian seasoning. Simmer for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti with the meat sauce until evenly coated. Serve hot with a dash of Parmesan on top.
